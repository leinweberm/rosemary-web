Rosemary

Through working in a bank, visiting monks in the Himalayas of Nepal, experiencing multiple personal rises and falls to finally finding a comfort in expressing my feelings and emotions on canvas or through photography. Passing control of my hands to whatever lies deep down within my subconsciousness, letting it flow freely in harmony with the tunes of my favorite music. It is not portrait, it is not landscape either, though people may see various things in it, but most importantly, it is me.
